Bazaar Walk through Pune Kasba

This tour takes you on an exciting yet knowledgeable journey through the oldest part of Pune. The area was part of the Maratha Empire and grew during the reign of Shivaji and the Peshwas.It has markets, temples, and places of cultural, religious and historical significance. The Shaniwar Wada was originally a grand palace where the Peshwas lived and looks very much like a fort. The original Lal Mahal (palace) was the residence of Shivaji Bhonsle/Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, and today its replica is seen here.

Activity Location: It is located in Pune, Maharashtra.



Sinhagad Fort

Located around 35 kilometres away from Pune in the Sahyadri Mountains, is this majestic 2000-year-old fortress. It houses the Kaundinya Eshwar Temple, some beautifully carved hidden caves, bastions, and tombs of Rajaram Chhatrapati and Tanaji Malusare.

Activity Location: Sinhagad Ghat Rd, Thoptewadi, Maharashtra 411025

Average cost : INR 999

Timing: 5 am to 6 pm
