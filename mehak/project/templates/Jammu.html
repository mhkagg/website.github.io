JAMMU&KASHMIR


Jammu and Kashmir is a tourist favorite for its scenic splendor, snow-capped mountains, plentiful wildlife, and local people handicrafts. Nestled in the Himalayan, Pir Panjal and Karakoram mountain ranges are renowned for their natural beauty and gentle people. Kashmir is memorable, as it's not only a nature lover's paradise but also a must-visit place for all adventure junkies. Kashmir's trekking and hiking routes are spellbinding, dotted with the most beautiful mountain peaks, massive glaciers, splendid monasteries, blue lakes, rivers, forests, verdant meadows, and tall pine trees.

ATTRACTIONS


Srinagar

Famously known as 'Heaven on Earth, Srinagar is located in the union territory of Jammu & Kashmir, on the banks of river Jhelum. Srinagar is known for the stationary houseboats and gondola-type rowboats- Shikaras on Dal Lake. Adorned with tranquil Dal Lake & Nigeen Lake, Srinagar is the dream destination for honeymoon and family holidays.

Must Visit: Dal Lake, Shankaracharya Temple, Nishat Bagh, Shikara Ride

Entry Fee : No entry fees

Ideal duration: 3-5 days



Vaishno Devi

Vaishno Devi is a temple town that's home to the famous Vaishno Devi Mandir. Located in Trikuta hills, 13 kms from Katra (in the union territory of Jammu and Kashmir); this renowned shrine allures millions of devotees from all over the world. Popularly known as Mata Rani, Vaishnavi and Trikuta, Vaishno Devi is a manifestation of the Hindu Goddess Durga.

Must Visit: Vaishno Devi Mandir, Katra, Ardhkuwari, Bhairavnath temple

Timings : Throughout the year

Entry Fee : No entry fees

Time Required : 1 day



Gulmarg

Situated at an altitude of 2730 m above sea level, Gulmarg is a popular skiing destination located in Pir Panjal Range of Union territory of Jammu and Kashmir. Surrounded by snow-covered lofty Himalayas, meadows of flowers, deep ravines, evergreen forested valleys, Gulmarg also has the world's second-highest Gondola ride.

Must Visit: Gulmarg Gondola, Kondori Phase1, Apharwat Peak, Drung Waterfall

Entry Fee : No entry fee

Ideal duration: 1-2 days



Patnitop

Patnitop it is a famous hill station in the Udhampur district of Jammu & Kashmir, known for endless meadows and panoramic views of the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas. Along with the widespread abundance in sceneries, Patnitop is known for activities like skiing and trekking as well as water springs.

Must Visit: Nathatop, Paragliding in Patnitop, Skiing in Patnitop, Naag Mandir

Best Time: Throughout the year

Ideal duration: 1-2 days
