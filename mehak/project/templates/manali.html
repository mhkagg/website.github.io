MANALI


In Hinduism, Manu was said to have survived a great flood that destroyed the rest of the world. He then recreated mankind in this Kullu Valley town. What a rush, huh? No wonder the towering peaks and verdant terrain of Manali attracts adventure travellers, with heli-skiing, hiking, mountaineering and river rafting the favored active pursuits. Come down from your endorphin high by breathing deeply at the four-story, wooden Hidimba Devi Temple, which sits in the middle of a nearby deciduous forest, or take a medicinal soak in the hot springs burbling from the ground a 30-minute walk from town

Paragliding In Manali

Take-off from the highest paragliding site in Manali based at a height of 8000 ft.Admire more than 5 majestical Himalayan peaks while gliding through the skies. Get a stunning video shot on a Go-Pro camera (optional) to capture some memories. Get guidance from an experienced instructor who will ensure a hassle-free experience

Activity Location: Dobhi, Manali

Average cost : INR 1,879

Flight Duration: 5-10 mins ( timings may vary )



River Rafting in Kullu

Kullu-Manali is a popular destination for its stunning mountains and lush green forests, as well as a variety of adventure sports. Amongst them, river rating is one of the most popular adventure activities conducted on the Beas river. Prepare to be enthralled by a thrilling river rafting adventure.

Activity Location: Beas River, Kullu

Average cost : INR 440

Rafting in Kullu Timings: 9:00 AM - 04:00 PM



Riverside Camping

Bestowed on a lush green foothill of the Pir Panjal range, this campsite is surrounded by a thick avenue of pine trees that overlooks the alluring waters of the Beas river.

Activity Location: Near Bahanu Bridge, Manali

Average cost : INR 1,089

Check-in time: 12:00 PM (Day 1)

Check-out time: 11:00 AM (Day 2)



Hot Air Balloon Ride in Manali

Hot air ballooning in Manali is a fun and exciting activity to take part in Manali. Indulging in this activity at this beautiful tourist hill station makes it even more attractive because of the picturesque views it offers.

Activity Location: Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Average cost : INR 1,149

Activity Duration: 15 minutes
